Important Notice

NOTICE

Dear Students,

This is to inform you that the Annual Sports Day will be held on Monday, 30th September, at the school playground starting from 9:00 AM. All students are required to be in their respective house uniforms and must assemble on time. Parents are cordially invited to attend and cheer for the participants.

The event will feature track events, team games, fun competitions, and award ceremonies. Refreshments will be provided to all students. Please make sure to bring your water bottles and caps, as the weather is expected to be sunny.

Students participating in events should report to their house captains for final practice sessions throughout this week. The list of participants is displayed on the notice board outside the main hall.

Kindly ensure maximum participation and cooperation to make this event a grand success. For any queries, please contact your class teacher or the Sports Department office.

Thank you for your attention and cooperation.
